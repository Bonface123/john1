Academics at Joy School International

Joy School International offers a rigorous academic program that prepares students for success in college and beyond. Our curriculum is designed to challenge students intellectually, develop critical thinking skills, and foster a lifelong love of learning.

Our Curriculum

We offer a comprehensive curriculum that includes:

  • Core subjects: English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies
  • Electives: Languages, Arts, Computer Science, Business, etc.
  • Advanced Placement (AP) and International Baccalaureate (IB) courses (optional)

Our Faculty

Our dedicated and experienced faculty members are passionate about their subjects and committed to student success. They provide individualized attention and support to help each student reach their full potential.

Learning Resources

Joy School International provides students with access to a variety of resources to support their learning, including:

  • Modern classrooms and laboratories
  • Well-stocked library with print and online resources
  • Technology integration in all classrooms
